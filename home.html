Welcome to Samtastic Shepherds, where my profound journey with German Shepherds spans over 12 years. My initial foray into ownership found me somewhat underprepared, marking the genesis of a substantial learning curve. Today, the breeding of German Shepherds has evolved into a passionate avocation, emphasizing a commitment to both the art and science of responsible breeding.

We specialize in breeding AKC registered, genetically tested, and high drive German Shepherds. Our goal is to provide healthy and well-tempered dogs that excel in various activities such as obedience, agility, and more.

With a master's in criminal justice, my dedication to the German Shepherd breed goes beyond a mere hobby. It is a deliberate effort to preserve the working line essence of these remarkable dogs. Samtastic Shepherds focuses on producing highly active dogs that challenge their owners on a daily basis, fostering an environment that aligns with the breed's heritage.

In addition to my role at the VA Medical Center, I embrace life's natural rhythms, with hiking, canning, and gardening. Samtastic Shepherds stands as a testament to my unwavering commitment to these exceptional animals and the principles that guide their breeding and upbringing.
